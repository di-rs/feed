Creative experiment building a Star Wars lightsaber duel animation rendered entirely in terminal using Unicode characters and GitHub Copilot CLI. Copilot acted as creative director suggesting motion trails, gradient lighting, shockwave effects, and molten damage, though camera rotation proved challenging with ASCII limitations requiring pivot to top-down views.
