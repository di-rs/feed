OpenAI for Government announces ChatGPT is joining the Department of Defense's secure GenAI.mil platform, used by 3 million civilian and military personnel. The custom ChatGPT deployment will help service members with tasks like document analysis, report generation, and administrative workflows while maintaining strict data isolation and security controls.
