A retired backend developer documents building a custom Arch Linux system from scratch, layering CachyOS optimized repositories, running dual kernels (linux-zen for daily work, linux-cachyos for gaming), and using BTRFS snapshots as a safety net on a rolling release. The focus is on understanding each layer of the stack rather than accepting defaults.
