Power BI enables analysts to transform messy, incomplete data into actionable insights through a layered approach: Power Query for ETL (cleaning, standardization), star schema for dimensional modeling, DAX for encoding business logic as standardized metrics, and intentional dashboard design following information hierarchy. Each layer builds on the previous one—clean data enables good models, good models enable good measures, and good measures with visuals enable good decisions—turning raw data into working decision tools that drive measurable business outcomes.
