A practical walkthrough of how data analysts use Power BI to transform raw data into actionable insights. Covers the complete workflow: sourcing data, cleaning with Power Query (handling missing records, duplicates, inconsistent formats), performing calculations with DAX functions (SUM, SUMX, CALCULATE), building visualizations, and presenting findings through dashboards and reports.
