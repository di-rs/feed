Power BI helps analysts transform messy organizational data into actionable insights through a structured workflow. The process involves data cleaning with Power Query, proper data modeling with fact and dimension tables, creating business logic with DAX measures, selecting appropriate visuals, and building decision-focused dashboards that highlight key trends and performance metrics. Each step depends on the previous one to ensure reliable insights that lead to informed business decisions.
