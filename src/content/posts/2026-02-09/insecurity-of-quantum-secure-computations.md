New research reveals limitations in quantum secure computation methods that were previously believed to provide complete privacy protection. While quantum technology can still support key sharing and certain applications, not all quantum-secure claims hold up under scrutiny, highlighting the need for layered protection and cautious evaluation of quantum security promises.
