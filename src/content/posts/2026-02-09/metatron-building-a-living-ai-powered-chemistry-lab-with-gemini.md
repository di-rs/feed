Metatron is an AI-powered chemistry platform that uses Google's Gemini to bridge theoretical chemistry with visual lab experiences. It features a virtual lab for real-time chemical reactions, an autonomous research agent that plans and executes full research workflows, voice-enabled assistance, and interactive learning tools.
