A detailed walkthrough of building Graphmotivo, a marketing intelligence platform using knowledge graphs and AI agentic workflows to generate buyer personas and ad targeting. Built in just 1.5 months using Cursor with Sonnet/Opus models (500m tokens), the project demonstrates end-to-end SaaS development from Neo4j graph database setup through production deployment with Docker, Supabase, and comprehensive monitoring.
