As AI becomes the primary tool for learning, Google highlights five ways to ensure safe, effective education: setting online/offline boundaries with Family Link, fostering critical thinking with Guided Learning in Gemini, helping teens spot AI content, involving parents through supervised accounts, and improving digital citizenship with Be Internet Awesome.
