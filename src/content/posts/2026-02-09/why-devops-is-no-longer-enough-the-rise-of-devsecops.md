Traditional DevOps optimized for speed but often ignored security, leading to vulnerabilities reaching production. DevSecOps embeds security directly into CI/CD pipelines as enforceable gates rather than post-deployment audits. The article walks through a complete DevSecOps pipeline including secrets scanning, SAST, dependency checks, container security, and runtime validation.
