Auditing your digital footprint takes under 30 minutes using five free methods: searching your name variations on Google, checking Have I Been Pwned for breach exposure, searching people-finder sites like Spokeo, reverse image searching your profile photos, and using OSINT platforms that aggregate data sources. Most people are surprised by how much is publicly accessible, making self-auditing a critical first step in personal security hygiene.
