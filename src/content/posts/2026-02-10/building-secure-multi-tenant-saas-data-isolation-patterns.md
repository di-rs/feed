This comprehensive guide explores three proven multi-tenant SaaS data isolation patterns based on production experience with thousands of organizations. Learn when to use shared database with row-level filtering, separate schemas per tenant, or dedicated databases, complete with implementation code, performance benchmarks from a healthcare platform, and trade-offs for security, scale, and operational complexity.
