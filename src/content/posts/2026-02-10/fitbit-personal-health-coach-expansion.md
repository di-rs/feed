Fitbit's AI-powered personal health coach, previously limited to eligible Premium users in the US, is now expanding to more countries including the UK, Canada, Australia, New Zealand, and Singapore. The public preview is also opening to iOS users in these regions simultaneously. Eligible users will receive an in-app update to access the feature and provide feedback.
