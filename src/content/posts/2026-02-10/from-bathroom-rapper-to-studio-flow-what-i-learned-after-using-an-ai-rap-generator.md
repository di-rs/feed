A personal account of using AI rap generators to bridge the gap between writing lyrics and hearing them performed, treating AI as a demo tool rather than a replacement for creativity. The author found that AI generators help identify weak spots in flow and cadence, making practice more efficient, though quality varies and they work best as a reference for self-recording.
