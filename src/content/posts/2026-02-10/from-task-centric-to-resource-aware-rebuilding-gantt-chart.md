Traditional Gantt charts model tasks well but fail to account for resource allocation across concurrent work, causing projects to slip despite looking correct on paper. Version 1.9.0 of this scheduling tool introduces a Resource View mode where rows represent people instead of tasks, with explicit percent-based allocation data, inline workload visualization, and automatic overload detection when capacity thresholds are exceeded.
