Google and YouTube announce updates for Safer Internet Day 2026, including easier parental controls in Family Link, improved screen time management for YouTube Shorts, and School time support for Android devices. New resources include an AI literacy guide for Be Internet Awesome and Guided Learning mode in Gemini to help kids think critically about AI-generated content.
