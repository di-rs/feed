A comprehensive guide on using Power BI to transform messy real-world datasets into actionable dashboards, using a hospital and pharmacy dataset as a practical example. The article walks through the full analytics workflow: Power Query for cleaning and deduplication, star schema data modeling to separate fact and dimension tables, writing DAX measures, and designing dashboards that answer actual business questions like patient volume and drug prescriptions.
