Real World Asset (RWA) tokenization is transforming how physical assets like real estate, bonds, commodities, and private equity are issued and governed by embedding ownership rights, compliance rules, settlement logic, and lifecycle management directly into blockchain smart contracts. The article explains the technical structure — SPVs, on-chain tokens, smart contract logic, custody, and identity layers — and how programmability replaces the manual, paper-heavy intermediary-driven systems of traditional asset management. This shift is reshaping institutional processes across both new digital-native markets and long-established asset classes.
