High availability in SaaS is engineered through four key pillars: real-time monitoring to catch anomalies before they become outages, auto-recovery systems that restart failed services and reroute traffic without human intervention, load balancing to absorb unpredictable traffic spikes, and regular backups to turn disasters into manageable incidents. The article frames reliability as a trust-building product feature, not just an infrastructure concern. Teams that invest in these patterns proactively avoid the 3 AM incident calls that plague reactive approaches.
