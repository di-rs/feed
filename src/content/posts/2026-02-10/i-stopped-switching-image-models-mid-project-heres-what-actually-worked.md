A developer recounts how constant model-hopping during a marketing sprint caused inconsistent AI-generated images and nearly missed a deadline. By committing to a single model (Ideogram V1) for layout consistency and building a reproducible pipeline with metrics and automated variant testing, they finally achieved reliable, shippable assets. The key lesson: lock in one model before optimizing prompts or upscaling workflows.
