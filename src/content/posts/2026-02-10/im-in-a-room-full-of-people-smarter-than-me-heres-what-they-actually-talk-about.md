A builder observes a Discord call with nine successful GTM leaders generating millions in revenue, and distills four recurring mental models they use: First Principles (reducing problems to their simplest form), the Horizon Problem (focusing on process over the ever-moving goalpost), and an emphasis on conversation as infrastructure for building. The key insight is that success at this level isn't about one playbook — it's about differentiated thinking applied through consistent frameworks.
