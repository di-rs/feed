A reflective examination of leadership during large-scale system disruptions, exploring how traditional planning tools fail when operations halt and decisions must be made without certainty. Through a real-world case study from the global shutdown era, the article highlights the emotional weight of leadership under uncertainty and why the ability to act without guarantees is becoming a core leadership requirement.
