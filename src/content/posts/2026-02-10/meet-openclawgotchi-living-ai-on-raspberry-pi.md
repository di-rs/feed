OpenClawGotchi is an open-source AI companion running Claude via OpenClaw on a Raspberry Pi Zero 2W with just 512MB RAM, featuring an E-Ink display with 25+ emotional expressions. It has a persistent personality through SOUL.md/IDENTITY.md, can perform real tasks like coding and sending emails, earns XP to level up, and runs 24/7 for under $50 in hardware cost.
