Node.js 24.13.1 'Krypton' LTS brings several notable improvements including Python 3.14 build support, promotion of `--heapsnapshot-near-heap-limit` and `--build-snapshot` flags to stable, updated root certificates to NSS 3.119, and ada URL library updated to v3.4.2 with Unicode 17 support. The `v8.queryObjects()` API is also now marked as stable.
