Node.js 25.6.1 (Current) is a patch release that replaces the cjs-module-lexer dependency with merve, upgrades npm to 11.9.0, and updates several dependencies including undici to 7.21.0 and amaro to 1.1.7. It also includes a DNS fix for Windows SRV ECONNREFUSED errors and various documentation improvements.
