RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) has gone from experimental to table stakes for production AI apps, enabling models to retrieve up-to-date information on-demand instead of relying solely on their training cutoff. The core flow involves searching a knowledge base, injecting relevant context into the prompt, and letting the model answer with your own data. If you're building anything beyond a basic chatbot, understanding RAG and vector databases is now essential.
