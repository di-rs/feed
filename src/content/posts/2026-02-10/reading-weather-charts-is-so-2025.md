The article argues that traditional marine weather apps showing raw data charts are outdated, requiring users to interpret complex meteorological information themselves. Instead, modern AI-powered APIs should provide simple GO/CAUTION/NO-GO answers with plain-English summaries tailored to the user's vessel and location. This shift from "here's the data, good luck" to "here's what it means for you" mirrors transformations in other industries like navigation and finance.
