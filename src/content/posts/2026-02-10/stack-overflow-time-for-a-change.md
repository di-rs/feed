An opinion piece examining how Stack Overflow's rigid Q&A format and often hostile community culture are pushing developers toward alternatives like DEV, Hashnode, and conversational AI tools. Annual developer survey data backs the trend, showing steady declines in question engagement as instant AI-powered answers become the norm. The author suggests the future of developer Q&A is a diverse ecosystem of communities rather than a single authoritative platform.
