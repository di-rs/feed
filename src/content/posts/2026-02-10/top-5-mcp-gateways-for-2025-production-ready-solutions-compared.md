Model Context Protocol (MCP) gateways provide the control plane needed to run AI agents in production, solving challenges around security isolation, observability, and centralized management that arise when connecting agents to external tools. This article compares five production-ready MCP gateway solutions — including Bifrost by Maxim AI — each with distinct architectural tradeoffs around performance, routing, and scalability. Choosing the right gateway depends on your team's needs around multi-tool orchestration, RBAC, connection pooling, and cost tracking.
