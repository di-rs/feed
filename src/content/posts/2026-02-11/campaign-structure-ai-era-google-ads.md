Google's Ads Decoded podcast explores how the traditional "more granularity = better" approach to Search ad campaign structures may now be counterproductive in the AI era. Product Management Director Brandon Ervin argues for simplified campaign structures that better leverage AI, emphasizing early-stage search intent and new controls that turn campaign setup into strategic levers. Advertisers clinging to overly granular multi-layered setups risk limiting the AI systems that power modern ad performance.
