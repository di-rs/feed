Smashing Magazine presents six concrete UX patterns for designing agentic AI systems that balance autonomy with user trust: Intent Preview, Autonomy Dial, Explainable Rationale, Confidence Indicators, Reversible Actions, and Audit Trails. The framework emphasizes that autonomy should feel like "a privilege granted by the user, not a right seized by the system," with design ensuring transparency and control at every stage of an agentic interaction lifecycle. This is the second installment in a series, moving from foundational theory to actionable design patterns and organizational practices.
