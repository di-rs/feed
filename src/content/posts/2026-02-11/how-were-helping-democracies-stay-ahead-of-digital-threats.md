At the Munich Security Conference, Google launched a new whitepaper "Staying Ahead of the Shadows: Digital Resilience in the Era of AI," outlining partnerships with defense organizations to help democracies build unified digital defenses. Google Threat Intelligence Group analysis highlights critical trends including state-sponsored actors targeting defense tech suppliers, with China running sophisticated campaigns against U.S. and European defense sectors. The initiative focuses on equipping governments with AI-powered security tools and resilience frameworks.
