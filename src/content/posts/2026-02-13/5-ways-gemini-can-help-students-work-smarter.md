Google outlines five ways Gemini is helping students this semester: free SAT and JEE Main practice tests with personalized feedback, writing refinement and presentation creation via Canvas, guided deep learning with interactive images and custom quizzes, resume and cover letter help, and mock interview preparation. Some features are also available as a free upgrade for college students through Gemini Advanced.
