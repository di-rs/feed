Google is opening applications for the Google for Startups Gemini Startup Forum focused on cybersecurity, a two-day London-based event created in partnership with Google DeepMind and Google Cloud. Selected Seed-to-Series-A founders will get hands-on access to Google AI, direct mentorship from Google engineers on scaling with Gemini, and long-term support through the Google for Startups program. Applications close April 29.
