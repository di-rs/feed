Google's Pixel Camera team showcased stunning photos taken at Lunar New Year celebrations in Jiufen, Taiwan using the Pixel 10 Pro, highlighting features like Pro Zoom, Panorama, Night Sight, and Long Exposure mode. The post also introduces Nano Banana Pro on Gemini for AI-powered image transformations, such as turning overcast scenes into vibrant sunlit landscapes. It's a visual showcase of the Pixel 10 Pro's camera capabilities ahead of the Year of the Horse.
