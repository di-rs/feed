The article explores multiple methods for programmatically generating unique Open Graph images in Ruby on Rails apps, covering DIY ImageMagick via MiniMagick and headless Chrome rendering via Grover/Puppeteer. Having distinct OG images per page significantly improves social media link preview click-through rates, and creating them manually in Figma doesn't scale beyond a handful of posts. Each approach has trade-offs between simplicity, visual quality, and security considerations.
