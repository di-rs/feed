Pinterest is fundamentally a search engine with 500M+ monthly users and 97% unbranded searches, making it a massive, underutilized SEO opportunity where well-placed pins can drive traffic for months. This guide walks through building a Pinterest keyword research tool that finds high-traffic keywords, analyzes what's ranking, and reverse-engineers competitor board strategies. The native Pinterest analytics are too limited for serious SEO work, so building custom tooling gives a significant edge.
