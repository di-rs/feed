A guide to 10 architectural principles for building production-grade Microsoft Power Platform solutions, emphasizing configuration-driven low-code approaches, modularity, zero trust security, and contextual design over cargo-culting trends. The principles cover Power Apps, Power Automate, Dataverse, and Dynamics 365 with concrete guidance like using child flows for reusable logic and preferring native business rules over custom JavaScript.
