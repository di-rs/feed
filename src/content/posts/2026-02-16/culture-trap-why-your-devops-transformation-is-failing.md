Organizations adopt all the visible artifacts of DevOps — CI/CD pipelines, Kubernetes, DORA metrics, daily standups — while the underlying culture remains unchanged: developers still throw code over the wall, post-mortems still devolve into blame sessions. The core failure is mistaking tools and rituals for culture itself, while the real drivers of behavior are the unspoken, taken-for-granted norms that no dashboard can fix.
