Most companies have adopted AI tools, but only a fraction of users — "Power Users" — extract their full potential, resulting in a massive productivity gap. Studies show the top 5% of users are multiple times more productive than average, with engineering teams saving 25–50 minutes per day through consistent AI use. The "Capability Overhang" describes this gap between what AI can do today and what most people actually leverage from it.
