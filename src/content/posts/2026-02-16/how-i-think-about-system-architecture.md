Before opening an editor, a seasoned developer walks through a mental checklist covering stack choice, performance and scalability bottlenecks, logging and observability, security and trust boundaries, failure control with graceful degradation, and budget/dependency risks. The core message: retrofitting good architecture is far more expensive than designing for it upfront.
