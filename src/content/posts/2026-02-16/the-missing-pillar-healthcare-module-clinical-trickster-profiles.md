Cisco's 2025 Cybersecurity Readiness Index found healthcare has the lowest AI threat awareness of any sector at 39%, largely because Shadow AI enters clinical workflows as convenience — speeding up documentation before quietly drifting. This article applies the Trickster Readiness Index to healthcare with four clinician-legible Shadow AI profiles (Documentation, Diagnostic, Communication, and Administrative tricksters) plus a weighted governance scoring model tuned for PHI-critical environments. An important read for anyone thinking about responsible AI deployment in regulated industries.
