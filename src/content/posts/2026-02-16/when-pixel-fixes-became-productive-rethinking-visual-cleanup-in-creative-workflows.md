Modern image AI tools are shifting visual cleanup from specialist retouchers to everyday creative pipelines, making object removal, watermark cleanup, and selective edits predictable and repeatable at scale. The key insight is that teams should redesign workflows around what can be safely automated, freeing humans to focus on higher-level decisions like composition and storytelling rather than pixel surgery. Reliability and repeatability matter more than raw accuracy when adopting AI image editing tools in production.
