Healthcare wastes $8.3 billion annually on administrative inefficiencies, with clinicians spending only 27% of their time on direct patient care. Modern agentic AI can transform entire workflows — automating insurance pre-authorizations, care coordination, and lab result alerts — with n8n connecting the disconnected EHR, billing, and portal systems that make automation possible.
