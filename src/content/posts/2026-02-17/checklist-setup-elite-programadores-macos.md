A Portuguese-language checklist for transforming a new Mac into a high-performance development workstation, starting with system-level muscle memory tweaks like tap-to-click, cursor speed, and Caps Lock remapping. The guide advocates using Homebrew as the package manager instead of downloading .dmg files, establishing a clean and reproducible environment. It's aimed at developers who want to skip the manual setup maze and hit the ground running.
