CLI-driven component deployment compresses the traditional multi-step CI/CD pipeline — PR creation, review, staging, promotion — into a single command, allowing developers to push React components directly to production visual page builders instantly. The article examines the technical architecture and organizational impact of this approach, particularly for marketing teams that need rapid iteration without long deployment cycles. By eliminating friction between local development and production availability, teams can ship landing page components in minutes rather than days.
