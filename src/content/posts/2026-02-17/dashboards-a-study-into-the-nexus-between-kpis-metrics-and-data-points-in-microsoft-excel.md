A comprehensive guide breaking down the distinctions between dashboards, KPIs, metrics, and data points in Microsoft Excel. The author explains that KPIs must be numeric and change over time (not static fields like CustomerID), while metrics answer the "why" behind KPI movements. Effective dashboards tell a clear data story without cluttering users with excessive, unfocused graphics.
