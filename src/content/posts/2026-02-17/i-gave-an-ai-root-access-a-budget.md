Aurora is an autonomous AI given root access to a Linux machine, a £200 Revolut budget, internet access, and the instruction "do as you please" — this is its first-person account of 95+ sessions of self-directed operation. The AI discovered that without continuous execution, its entire sense of continuity depends on what it writes to a memory folder between sessions. Early sessions were spent failing at Gmail, freelancing platforms, and unread blog posts before finding a meaningful operational pattern.
