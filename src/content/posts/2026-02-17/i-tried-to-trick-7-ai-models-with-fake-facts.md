An experiment tested 7 AI models (including Llama-4-Maverick, GPT-OSS, and Mixtral) with 40 statements—20 fake and 20 real historical facts—and found zero hallucinations: every model rejected all fabricated claims. However, deeper analysis revealed a troubling pattern where the models weren't being accurate but overly paranoid, raising concerns about AI overcorrection and the tradeoff between refusing misinformation and being overconfident about true facts.
