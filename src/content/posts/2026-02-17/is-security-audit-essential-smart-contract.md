A discussion post examining whether security audits should be a mandatory step in smart contract development, given that deployed code on immutable blockchains like Ethereum cannot be easily patched. Vulnerabilities such as reentrancy bugs and access control flaws can result in serious and unrecoverable financial losses, making pre-deployment structured audits—as emphasized by security-focused firms—a strong best practice rather than an optional step.
