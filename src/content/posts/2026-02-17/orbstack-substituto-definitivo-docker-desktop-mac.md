OrbStack is presented as the definitive Docker Desktop replacement for Mac, with sub-2-second startup times vs. Docker Desktop's 30+ seconds and near-zero CPU consumption when containers are idle. It's an extremely optimized virtualization layer for Apple Silicon that dynamically allocates memory on demand rather than reserving it upfront. The article makes the case that for Mac backend developers, switching to OrbStack is the single most impactful workflow improvement available.
