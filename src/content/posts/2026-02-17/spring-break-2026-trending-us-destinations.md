Google Search reveals the top 10 trending U.S. spring break destinations for 2026, led by Hilo, Hawaii, with Florida's west coast claiming five of the spots including Sarasota, Panama City, and Fort Myers. The list was compiled by analyzing the largest percentage increase in Google Flights searches for domestic trips in March and April 2026 versus the same period in 2025. Google's latest AI upgrades in Search make it easy to build custom itineraries, find affordable flights, and complete bookings in one place.
