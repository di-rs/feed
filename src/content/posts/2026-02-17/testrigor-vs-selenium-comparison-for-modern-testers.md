A detailed breakdown comparing TestRigor and Selenium for modern test automation, contrasting Selenium's deep flexibility and code-centric approach with TestRigor's AI-driven plain-English test creation that lowers the barrier for non-programmers. Selenium remains the industry standard for web apps requiring full framework control, while TestRigor extends coverage to web, mobile, desktop, and APIs with reduced maintenance overhead. The right tool depends on team skillset, application complexity, and whether reduced maintenance effort outweighs the loss of low-level control.
