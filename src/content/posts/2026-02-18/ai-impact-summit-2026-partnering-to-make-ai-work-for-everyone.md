At the AI Impact Summit in India, Google announced a sweeping set of global partnerships, infrastructure investments, and innovation programs aimed at making AI accessible to everyone. Highlights include a $15B investment in India's AI infrastructure, the America-India Connect fiber-optic initiative, and new $30M Google.org challenges targeting AI for science and government. The announcements span workforce skills, climate tech, and frontier AI research partnerships with Indian institutions.
