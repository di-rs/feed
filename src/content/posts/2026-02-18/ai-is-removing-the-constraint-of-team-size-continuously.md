AI tools are progressively eliminating team size as a constraint in software development and business, enabling small teams to accomplish what previously required large organizations. As AI handles more tasks autonomously, professionals and companies need proactive strategies to adapt to rapidly shifting workforce dynamics. The author, an AI strategist and founder of ReThynk AI, challenges developers and leaders to start thinking now about how they'll evolve alongside these capabilities.
