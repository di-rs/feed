Not every product problem needs AI — this article gives PMs a practical framework to choose between deterministic automation and probabilistic AI. Deterministic systems produce the same output every time (rules like "if cart > $100 → free shipping"), while AI trades certainty for adaptability on ambiguous inputs like spam detection or recommendations. A hybrid approach often makes the most sense when clear rules apply but edge cases require reasoning.
