AceHack 5.0 is Rajasthan's largest student-run hackathon and the region's first official Major League Hacking partner, organized by Rakesh Bangra and the ACM Student Chapter at UEM Jaipur. Under the theme "FRACTURED REALITY," it challenges participants to break conventional thinking and build solutions that reshape real-world problems. The article gives a behind-the-scenes look at how a college hackathon grew into a national-level student innovation platform.
