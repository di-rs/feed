Google unveiled the Pixel 10a, a $499 phone powered by the custom Tensor G4 chip with an improved camera system, brighter screen, and faster charging than the Pixel 9a. It features 30+ hours of battery life, a 100% recycled aluminum frame with satin finish, and comes in four colors: Lavender, Berry, Fog, and Obsidian. The phone is available for pre-order now.
