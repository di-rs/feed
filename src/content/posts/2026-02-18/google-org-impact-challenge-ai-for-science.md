Google.org is launching a $30 million global open-call Impact Challenge to fund researchers, nonprofits, and social enterprises using AI to drive scientific breakthroughs. Priority areas include Health and Life Sciences, Crisis Resilience, and Environmental Science, with selected organizations also receiving engineering support and Google infrastructure via the Google.org Accelerator. Applications are open until April 17, 2026.
