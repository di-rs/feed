In this PodRocket episode, Russ Miles argues that developer platforms fail when treated as factories rather than habitats for meaningful work — every org already has an internal platform, it's just accidental without a product mindset. Key insights: optimize cognitive burden rather than hiding complexity, don't let "shift left" become "dump left" without providing decision support, and recognize that tightening the observe→decide→act feedback loop is the real platform feature.
