An email developer who'd spent years writing HTML tables and inline CSS used Claude Code to build a complete AI-powered fridge-to-recipe app in under a week, including AI vision, payments, and real users. With no full-stack background and no budget for engineers, she shipped a product that photographs your fridge and suggests recipes prioritized by what's about to expire. The story is a compelling demonstration of how LLM coding tools are enabling solo creators to ship products that previously required entire engineering teams.
