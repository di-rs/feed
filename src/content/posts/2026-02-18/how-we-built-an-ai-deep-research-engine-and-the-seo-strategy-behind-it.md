Neiro is an AI-powered deep research engine that scans thousands of live sources per query, cross-references data points, and delivers structured intelligence dossiers with full citations — targeting analysts, researchers, and product managers who need answers beyond a quick search. The technical architecture enforces zero hallucinations by making every data point traceable to its source, outputting structured results with executive summaries, data tables, and competitive matrices. The post also details the startup's organic SEO strategy for building visibility in the AI tools space.
