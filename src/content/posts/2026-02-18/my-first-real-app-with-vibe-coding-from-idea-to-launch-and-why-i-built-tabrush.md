TabRush, a Safari tab ad marketplace giving sponsors a dedicated spotlight placement, was shipped using a vibe coding workflow from concept to launch prep. Tight prompt constraints and short iteration loops worked well; broad prompts produced generic outputs that still needed human product judgment. The key lesson: AI coding gives speed, but clarity, positioning, and taste still come from the founder.
