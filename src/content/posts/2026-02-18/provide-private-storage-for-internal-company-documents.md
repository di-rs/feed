A practical walkthrough of setting up private Azure Cloud Storage accounts for internal company documents, including redundancy configuration for high availability during regional outages. The tutorial covers creating storage accounts, configuring shared access signatures for restricted partner access, backing up public website storage, and implementing lifecycle management to move content to cheaper storage tiers. Ideal for organizations needing secure, highly-available private document infrastructure.
