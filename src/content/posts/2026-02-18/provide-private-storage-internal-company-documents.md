A step-by-step guide to setting up private Azure Blob Storage for internal company documents, ensuring data is restricted to authorized users with proper authentication and access controls. The walkthrough covers creating a storage account in the Azure portal, configuring resource groups for high availability, and ensuring the storage is not publicly accessible over the internet. Suitable for teams needing to securely store proprietary or sensitive business documents in the cloud.
