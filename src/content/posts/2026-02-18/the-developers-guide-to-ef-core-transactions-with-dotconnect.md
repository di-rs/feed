A deep dive into EF Core transaction management using Devart dotConnect providers, covering implicit vs. explicit transactions, ACID principles, and advanced scenarios like distributed transactions and multi-database workflows. Dotconnect enables seamless sharing of connections and transaction scopes between EF Core and raw ADO.NET commands, plus support for savepoints for granular rollback. Essential reading for .NET developers building reliable multi-step database operations.
