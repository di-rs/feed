Data modeling is the process of defining entities, attributes, and relationships to create a structured blueprint for databases and data platforms. It operates at three levels — conceptual (business stakeholders), logical (data architects), and physical (database engineers) — each serving a distinct audience and purpose. Getting data models right ensures consistent shared understanding across dashboards, reports, AI agents, and applications.
