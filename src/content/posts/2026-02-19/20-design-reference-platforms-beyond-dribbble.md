Beyond mainstream platforms like Dribbble and Behance, this article reviews 20 alternative design reference platforms that offer video recordings, Figma files, real user flows, and component code rather than just static images. From Mobbin's mobile UX pattern library to SaaSFrame's complete SaaS journey documentation, these tools help designers understand what actually works in production environments. Resources like Awwwards, Component Gallery, and Refero provide deeper insights into design evolution, award-level execution, and system-level component thinking.
