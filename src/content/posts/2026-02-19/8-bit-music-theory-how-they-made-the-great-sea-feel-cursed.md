A video essay breakdown of how Wind Waker's iconic "Great Sea" theme was transformed from adventurous to deeply unsettling through deliberate musical mischief. Composers introduced tritones and clashing harmonies to warp the familiar tune, while subtly weaving in Ganondorf's ominous motif to add an extra layer of dread. It's a great example of how specific note choices can completely flip the emotional register of a well-known piece of music.
