Amazon EKS is a fully managed Kubernetes service from AWS that handles the operational complexity of running Kubernetes—including control plane setup, maintenance, and scaling—so teams can focus on their applications. It integrates natively with AWS services like IAM, VPC, CloudWatch, and Auto Scaling, making enterprise-grade container orchestration accessible without deep Kubernetes expertise. The article walks through EKS architecture, key benefits, common use cases, and step-by-step instructions to get started.
