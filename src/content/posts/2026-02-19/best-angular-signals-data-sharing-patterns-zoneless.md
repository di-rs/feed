A practical exploration of Angular's signal-first architecture in zoneless mode, comparing signals, RxJS subjects, and TanStack Query for cross-component state sharing. Signals enable precise, subscription-free reactivity that's reportedly 3x faster than Zone.js-based change detection with smaller bundles. The article covers real enterprise-scale patterns for eliminating Zone.js overhead while maintaining clean, maintainable reactive data flows.
