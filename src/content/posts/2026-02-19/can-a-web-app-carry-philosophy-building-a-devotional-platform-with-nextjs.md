The developer built Sarathi, a devotional self-growth platform inspired by the Bhagavad Gita in Next.js, prioritizing emotional immersion over typical content-site aesthetics through restrained color palettes, depth-based layering, and intentional typography. Key engineering lessons include structuring 700+ static verses with static generation, precomputing data to avoid over-fetching, and the insight that designing for emotion is harder than designing for usability.
