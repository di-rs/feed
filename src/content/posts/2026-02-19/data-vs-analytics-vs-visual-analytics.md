Data, analytics, and visual analytics are often treated as interchangeable but serve fundamentally different roles: raw data tells you what happened, analytics explains why, and visual analytics translates insights into narratives that guide confident decisions at scale. Most organizations drown in data but starve for clarity because they treat data access as the end goal rather than the starting point. Building systems that progress through all three layers is the foundation for business intelligence that actually drives action.
