A solo developer built "Eggbert," an AI ops agent running on OpenClaw that manages two Twitter accounts, a LinkedIn content calendar with human-in-the-loop approval via Telegram buttons, and even writes blog posts in the developer's own voice — including this very post. Twice a week a cron job reads context files about recent work and publishes to Hashnode and Dev.to, raising interesting questions about authorship and automation in the age of AI.
