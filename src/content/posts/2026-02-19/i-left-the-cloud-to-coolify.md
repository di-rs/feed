After years of escalating AWS bills and managing cloud complexity, a developer migrated to Coolify—an open-source self-hosting platform—to regain control and reduce costs. The author argues traditional cloud is just "someone else's computer" with pay-as-you-go pricing that quietly grows, while Coolify offers a simpler self-hosted alternative. The move trades managed convenience for cost transparency and full control over your own infrastructure.
