A comprehensive guide to mastering Git for production environments, covering branching, merging strategies, squash commits, and rebase operations through hands-on examples. Engineers learn how to create isolated branches, resolve merge conflicts, condense commit history, and push completed projects to GitHub. Understanding these workflows is critical for maintaining a stable and traceable codebase in collaborative team settings.
