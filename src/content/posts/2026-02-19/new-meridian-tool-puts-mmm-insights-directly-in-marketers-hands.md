Google has introduced Scenario Planner, a no-code interface for its open-source Marketing Mix Model (MMM) tool Meridian, enabling marketers to experiment with budget scenarios and see real-time ROI estimates without data science expertise. This directly addresses the finding that nearly 40% of marketers struggle to connect MMM outputs to real business decisions, making analytics insights accessible across teams regardless of technical background.
