A comprehensive guide to using Amazon GuardDuty and Amazon Inspector together for a robust DevSecOps strategy. GuardDuty provides 24/7 intelligent threat detection across EC2, EKS, Fargate, S3, RDS, and even backup storage, with runtime protection and automated malware scanning. Inspector handles vulnerability management, making the two services complementary pillars of AWS cloud security embedded throughout the development lifecycle.
