A comprehensive 2026 comparison of three major UI approaches — Tailwind CSS (utility-first), MUI (Material Design component library), and Ant Design (enterprise design system) — analyzing their philosophies, performance, scalability, and fit with AI-assisted development. The post argues that choosing the wrong UI strategy can slow a product for years, and walks through real-world trade-offs for SaaS products, dashboards, and enterprise apps. Understanding the core philosophy behind each tool is framed as more important than comparing feature checklists.
