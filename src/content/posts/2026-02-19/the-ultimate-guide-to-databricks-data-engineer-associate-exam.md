A comprehensive study guide for the Databricks Data Engineer Associate certification, covering Apache Spark fundamentals, Delta Lake, ELT pipelines, Delta Live Tables, Databricks Workflows, Unity Catalog, and performance optimization. Databricks is a unified analytics platform built on Apache Spark by its original creators, running on AWS, Azure, and GCP, combining data engineering, science, ML, and BI into one ecosystem. The guide includes practice questions and exam tips spanning all major exam domains from security and access control to incremental data processing.
