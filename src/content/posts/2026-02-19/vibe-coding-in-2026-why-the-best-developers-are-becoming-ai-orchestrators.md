"Vibe coding" — describing what you want and letting AI write the code — is emerging as the dominant developer workflow in 2026, enabling projects that once took two weeks to be completed in a day. Strong fundamentals remain critical however, as AI confidently produces wrong code that only knowledgeable developers can catch. The best engineers are evolving into AI orchestrators: handling system design and the "why" while AI handles the "how."
