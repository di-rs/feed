This German-language guide explains why selecting the right search engine within the Tor network is critical for maintaining anonymity, even though Tor itself encrypts traffic through three relay nodes. Many conventional search engines continue to log metadata and build user profiles even when accessed via Tor, creating a contradiction that undermines the privacy the network is supposed to provide. The article outlines the key privacy criteria to evaluate when choosing a secure Tor-compatible .onion search engine to protect identity and browsing history within the darknet.
