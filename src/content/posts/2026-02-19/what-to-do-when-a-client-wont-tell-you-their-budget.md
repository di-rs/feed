73% of freelancers face clients who refuse to disclose budgets upfront, forcing blind proposals that waste time or price you out of work. The article breaks down why clients hide budgets (fear of overpaying, negotiation tactics, or genuine ignorance about market rates) and offers practical counter-strategies including tiered proposals, anchored pricing, and framing budget disclosure as mutually beneficial. Treating budget conversations as project scoping rather than negotiation changes the dynamic entirely.
