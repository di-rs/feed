While 73% of newsrooms now use AI for writing and content personalization, automated AI fact-checking remains an underexplored application with potentially the highest journalistic impact. India's sprawling media ecosystem — 100,000+ publications in 20+ languages — makes manual fact-checking impossible at scale, and AI systems using NLP for claim extraction can help fill the gap. The article explores how modern AI fact-checking works through claim identification, cross-referencing knowledge databases, and source credibility assessment to combat misinformation at the speed it spreads.
