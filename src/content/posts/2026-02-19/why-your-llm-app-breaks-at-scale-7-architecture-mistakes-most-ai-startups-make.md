A deep dive into seven architecture mistakes that cause LLM applications to collapse under production load, including treating LLM APIs like deterministic REST services, ignoring token economics at scale, and lacking response caching strategies. The article argues that scaling failures are almost never the model's fault — they stem from missing async handling, poor observability, no cost guardrails, and over-reliance on prompt engineering instead of structured retrieval.
